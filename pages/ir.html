顏盟峯教授


Stephane Meng-Feng Yen is an associate professor at Department of Accountancy and Graduate Institute of Finance in National Cheng-Kung University (NCKU).He is also the director at the Research Center of Financial Innovation and Investments, NCKU since July, 2013.

Yen specializes in hedge funds, financial econometrics, trading strategies, and socially responsible investment.
